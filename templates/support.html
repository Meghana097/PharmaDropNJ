Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I find a drop-off location?
    You can find drop-off locations using the 'Find Locations' feature in the app or by visiting our Locations Page.
  • What should I do if I have a large quantity of medications to dispose of?
    Please contact us directly for assistance with large quantities.
  • Can I ship my medications?
    Yes, you can ship it to your nearest location for safe disposal of your medications.
